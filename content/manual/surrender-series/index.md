INTELLIGENT SEQUENCINGThe Yoga Flow Yoga C1 Sequence is designed to give our students a well rounded introductory yoga experiencein 60 minutes. The intention and order of the postures hold within themmany of the fundamental lessons onintelligent yoga sequencing.
